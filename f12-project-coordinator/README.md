# IT Project Coordinator | F12.net

**Duration:** October 2023 – March 2024  
**Environment:** MSP — multi-client IT project management  
**Location:** Vancouver, BC

## Role Overview

After two years as a Project Technician building deep technical experience across F12's client base, I transitioned into the IT Project Coordinator role. This shift moved me from executing on-site technical work to owning the end-to-end delivery of infrastructure projects — managing scope, schedule, budget, resources, and client communication.

This role required applying the Google Project Management Certificate skills in a real, fast-paced MSP environment where multiple projects run simultaneously across different clients, each with unique requirements and stakeholders.

## Key Responsibilities

- Managed the full lifecycle of IT infrastructure projects from initial scoping through post-implementation review
- Built and maintained project roadmaps, timelines, and resource allocation plans
- Served as the primary point of contact for client stakeholders throughout project delivery
- Coordinated with vendor partners on procurement, scheduling, and SLA management
- Managed project budgets and tracked actuals against estimates
- Facilitated cross-functional collaboration between technical teams, sales, and client contacts
- Conducted post-implementation reviews and documented KPIs and lessons learned

## Technologies Used

- Project management tools — scheduling, Gantt, resource tracking
- ConnectWise Manage — project tracking and ticket management
- IT Glue — documentation and runbook management
- Microsoft Teams and SharePoint — internal and client collaboration
- Vendor portals — procurement and SLA management
